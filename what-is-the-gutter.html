The Gutter

When you visit a website you may have already given up some of your personal data depending on how you accessed that site.

 

When you visit a site your IP address and cookies will all be available to that site and to the 3rd party scripts that run on it. This is enabled by default and allows sites like Facebook to view every site you visit using cookies and collect other data about you (Only if a Facebook script is runs that site).

 

 Have you ever got ads on something that you were just looking at? The advertisers have access to your cookies allowing them to target their ads to you increasing their revenue.
